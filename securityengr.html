While developing a software, analysis of its both functional as well as non functional requirements should be done in early stages. Among all non-functional requirements security requirements are the most important one.

Compromise on software security may result in catastrophic failure of the system causing huge loss of valuable assets.

This workshop will highlight the tools, techniques and methods needed to assist in the development and maintenance of systems that are
resistant to malicious activities.
